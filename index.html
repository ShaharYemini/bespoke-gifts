THE ART OF THE PHOTO MOSAIC

My specialty is the bespoke photo mosaic. You provide two things: a single, meaningful 'target' photo (like a portrait or a favorite landscape), and a collection of your smaller, personal photos (the memories). I then meticulously arrange your memories to artistically recreate the target photo. The result is more than a gift; it's an interactive piece of art where every detail tells a part of your story.
